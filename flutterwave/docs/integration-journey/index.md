## Integration Journey

Understand the Integration journey before you commence.

![](https://files.readme.io/eea775ca8ad1c1591b197db7947a5fe6e94f4d5cd7198403706e238117c69ce6-3db7decd82b17d0b411e8ca33c22914fde5399dff9eb47f7fa67ae63b856a19c-API_Doc_Sample.png)

To integrate Flutterwave into your application, follow this step-by-step guide.

Sign up for a free developer [account](https://onboarding.flutterwave.com/signup/steps/670fd6ca31db5a18fd7d03a7). This gives you access to our sandbox environment, where you can test and simulate all our features.

In the sandbox (test) environment, you will be able to:

1.  Receive payments using mock account data.
2.  Mock bank and mobile money transfers.
3.  Simulate webhooks and errors

Learn more about testing in the [sandbox](https://developer.flutterwave.com/v4.0/docs/testing).

Flutterwave supports multiple integration options depending on your stack and needs:

1.  Flutterwave standard - Generate Flutterwave checkout UI using the standard API.
2.  Libraries (SDKs) and Plugins
3.  Direct API integrations - Process payment from your server using Flutterwave's endpoints.

> ## 📘
> 
> New to APIs?
> 
> [Read this beginner-friendly guide](https://flutterwave.com/us/blog/getting-started-with-apis) to understand how APIs work and how to use them in your app.

Once you’ve picked an integration method, test it thoroughly using sandbox credentials and mock data. We’ve compiled [common errors](https://developer.flutterwave.com/v4.0/docs/common-errors) and [best practices](https://developer.flutterwave.com/v4.0/docs/best-practices) to help you troubleshoot issues early.

After completing your integration, sign up for a production [account](https://onboarding.flutterwave.com/signup/steps/645ccc96f618c2250a85af46). This account gives you access to actual transaction processing capabilities.

To start making live transactions, you will need to complete your KYC verification process, which involves submitting a copy of your business registration and [other documents](https://flutterwave.com/ng/support/general/what-are-the-requirements-for-using-flutterwave).

Once your account is verified and approved, you're ready to start accepting real payments in 30+ currencies from your Flutterwave dashboard.

Before you start transacting:

1.  **Explore your dashboard**: It has more settings and features than the test mode. [Watch our quick demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p5qK5UqriVo&list=PLdQn4TrYwb0MOmOZykrznNHNy0XI0pC8k&index=1&t=1s) to learn your way around.
2.  **Review your integration**: Use our **go-live checklist** to double-check everything works as expected.
3.  **Transfer your settings**: Apply the same configurations (webhooks, redirect URLs, allowed domains, etc.) from test mode to your live account
