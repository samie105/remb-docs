## Unify and manage your business across multiple accounts.

Centralize reporting, operations, and team management across your enterprise

Build an organization to maintain separate business entities under consolidated ownership and access.

[](https://docs.stripe.com/get-started/account/orgs/build)

![](https://b.stripecdn.com/docs-statics-srv/assets/orgs-hero.e513e54ff276a07f9b0e8f6ce427de50.png)

Simplify your operations and account management using Organizations. This feature provides a centralized view in the [Stripe Dashboard](https://dashboard.stripe.com/org/dashboard) of all of your business lines or subsidiaries. You can use it to:

*   Gauge your entire business’ performance.
*   See all of your [transactions](https://docs.stripe.com/dashboard/basics#transactions), [disputes](https://docs.stripe.com/disputes/responding), [invoices](https://docs.stripe.com/invoicing/dashboard/manage-invoices), [connected accounts](https://docs.stripe.com/connect/dashboard/viewing-all-accounts), [customers](https://docs.stripe.com/dashboard/basics#customers), and more.
*   Download unified reports across currencies.
*   Streamline team management and SSO from a centralized location.
*   Perform custom SQL queries with [Sigma](https://docs.stripe.com/stripe-data/how-sigma-works) across all accounts.

## Use cases

Operating across multiple Stripe accounts is a common practice for many businesses. Consider the following scenarios:

Scenario

Description

**Global expansion**

Create separate Stripe accounts for each country or region to take advantage of local acquiring.

**Separate business units**

Create separate Stripe accounts for each independent business unit to isolate operations and finances.

**Franchise groups**

Centrally manage franchise locations, represented as connected accounts under a platform.

**Company acquisitions**

Acquire another business that uses Stripe.
